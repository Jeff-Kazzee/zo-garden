# Prompt name

## Inputs

- `{topic}` — the subject to research
- `{depth}` — short | medium | deep

## Prompt

Research {topic} at {depth} depth. Return:

1. A one-paragraph summary
2. Three concrete examples
3. Two caveats or limitations

Do not pad. If a section is thin, say so.
